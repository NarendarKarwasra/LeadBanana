We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name

  • Email address
  • Business name
  • City or location
  • Service interest
  • Any details you submit through forms, demo requests, or contact inquiries

Business Information

  • Company details shared during inquiries or onboarding
  • Service requirements
  • Team size or business needs, where relevant

Technical Information

  • IP address

  • Website usage data

We may use your information to:
